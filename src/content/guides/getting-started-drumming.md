The best beginner setup is one that keeps you practicing consistently, not the most expensive rig.

## Core gear I recommend

- **Practice pad + stand**: low noise, easy to use daily, and perfect for building stick control.
- **Metronome app**: your timing improves faster when every session has a steady pulse.
- **Drum throne**: comfort matters more than people expect, especially for longer practice sessions.
- **In-ear isolation or ear protection**: protect your hearing from day one.

## Online resources worth your time

- **YouTube channels focused on fundamentals** for grip, strokes, and timing.
- **Play-along tracks with click** so you can apply grooves in musical context.
- **Structured beginner lesson playlists** that build topics in sequence.

## How to use this list

Start simple, commit to short daily sessions, and only upgrade gear after consistency is established.
