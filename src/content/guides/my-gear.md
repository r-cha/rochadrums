Every part of my setup is chosen for consistency, flexibility, and fast session workflow.

## Why this setup works for me

- **Reliable shell pack**: tuned to adapt quickly between worship, rock, and heavier genres.
- **Balanced cymbal selection**: enough brightness for cut, with darker options for texture.
- **Session-ready hardware**: stable stands and pedal response I can trust in both live and studio settings.
- **Simple recording chain**: clean source tone first, then minimal processing where needed.

## My approach to choosing gear

I prioritize feel and repeatable sound over trends. If a piece of gear helps me serve the song and keeps setup friction low, it stays.
